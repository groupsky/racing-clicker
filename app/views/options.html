Where is RCM's data saved?

RCM saves your progress in your browser's localstorage, cookies, and Flash (SWF) storage. Your saved game will be loaded if it's found in any one of these sources. This is done to avoid accidentally losing your saved data; it's not intended to track you or otherwise invade your privacy. The 'wipe all saved data' button below will clear all three storage locations.
